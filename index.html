About Our Golf Club

Our golf facility in the Orangeville area offers a full range of golf programs and lessons for Adults, Ladies, and Juniors. We take pride in the care and maintenance of our golf course and always strive to ensure our guests have a pleasant experience.

We have an outdoor patio and will be offering food and beverage services! Dufferin Glen Golf Club is a licensed establishment, outside alcohol is strictly prohibited.

Located in the historical town of Orangeville, Ontario, Dufferin Glen Golf Club is nestled within mature trees and rolling hills. The course layout/condition will have you falling in love, have you begging for more, and is a great test for any and all skill levels.
